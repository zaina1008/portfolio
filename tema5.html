ECHO

This project focused on redesigning a company’s website and creating new content to strengthen its visual identity. Working as part of a team, we developed a fresh and engaging digital experience that better reflected the company’s personality and brand.

From Design to Development

Throughout the project, I worked with website redesign, content creation, photography, and visual storytelling. Creating new content and improving the overall design helped me see how visuals, branding, and layout work together to create a more engaging experience. It also gave me a better understanding of how design can communicate a brand’s personality and connect with its audience.

Reflection

This project gave me hands-on experience working with real content and brand communication. It helped me understand how design, imagery, and content work together to shape a company’s identity and create a consistent experience for users across a website.
