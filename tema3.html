Emnesite

The project helped me understand how research and testing can make a website better. By working with moodboards, wireframes, prototypes, and user feedback, I learned how design decisions can be guided by what users actually need and experience.

From Design to Development

This project focused on the UX/UI design process, from research and idea development to testing and prototyping. Through user research, wireframing, and visual design, I created a website based on user needs and insights rather than assumptions. Working through each stage of the design process helped me understand how research can guide design decisions and contribute to a more user-friendly and effective final product.

Reflection

This project showed me how important UX is in the design process. It helped me understand that good design is about more than just making something look nice—it’s also about understanding users and creating a better experience for them. The skills I gained during this project still influence the way I approach design today.
