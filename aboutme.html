About me

Hi, I'm Zainab, a Multimedia Design student who enjoys turning ideas into websites. What I like most about multimedia design is coding and seeing a design come to life. There's something really satisfying about starting with a simple idea and building it into a finished website.

When I design, I focus on creating websites that are clean, easy to use, and visually appealing. I believe good design should feel simple and intuitive while still having personality.

Through my projects, I enjoy combining design and coding to create digital experiences that look good, work well, and leave a positive impression.
